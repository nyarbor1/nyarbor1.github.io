Website Evaluations

The Room Movie Website

This is a website showing off a movie called "The Room". It is not impressive visually and it is no surprise that it does not follow the CRAP design principles.

  • Contrast - There is not good contrast between the items in the nav menu (which are red) and the background. Some users may find this hard if not impossible to read.
  • Repetition - Nothing on this page looks uniform, it creates a feeling that the company responsible is not very professional.
  • Alignment - This page is not well aligned at all. It feels like the different elements on the page have no structure whatsoever.
  • Proximity - The various elements on the page are so close to one another. It is hard to tell where one element ends and the other starts.

UNC Charlotte Website

The Charlotte Website does a great job at utilizing CRAP design principles. The aesthetics of the site are good, nothing seems out of place, and they did a good job at making the right things pop out at you.

  • Contrast - There is a lot of good contrast on this page. Nothing is hard to read because it is too similar to the background
  • Repetition - There is just the right amount of repitition. Elements that look alike provide similar functionality.
  • Alignment - The alignment on Charlotte's webpage is good, everything that should be together is and elements that are vastly different are separated.
  • Proximity - All related elements are close to each other while being spaced properly for minimal confusion.
