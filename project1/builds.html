ADC Builds

Items

Hunters Cowl, Devourers Gauntlet, Demon Blade, Dominance, Deathbringer, Bladed Boomerang

Crit (Most Popular)

This is generally most popular and recomended because it gives you the best balance of attack speed, life steal, ease of use, while maintaining a high enough crit chance to be consistant. It also tends to be easiest to carry your games as without crit you can sometimes find yourself leaning heavily on your team mates always doing their jobs, which as we all know, doesnt always happen.

Items

Hunters Cowl, Devourers Gauntlet, Executioner, Quins Sais, Dominance, Silverbranch Bow.

Non Crit (Most Consistent)

This build is best for shredding tanks and easier to hit attack speed cap, while also being far more consistant than relying on crit RNG. you'll usually pivot to this build when tank heavy metas are more common.

Items

Hunters Cowl, Devourers Gauntlet, Demon Blade, Executioner, Deathbringer, Quins Sais.

Hybrid DPS (Most DPS)

This build is almost always the most possible total DPS you can get but also usually only uses 1 or 2 crit items. While this is excellent for competitive scenarios, i generally avoid this as luck plays a major factor in 1v1s around the map, as well as team fights, where you can get very unlucky and lose games because of it.

Items

Deaths Toll, Transcendence, Hydras Lament, Deathbringer, Failnot, Dominance, Sell DT for Bloodforge or Demon Blade

Burst DPS

This is a very specialized build path not seeing much use as an ADC very often. Its more of a high skill high reward type of playstyle with glaring downsides where it struggles. i wouldnt generally recommend this style of play unless you plan on pretty much exclusivly diving back line and blowing people up very quickly.
